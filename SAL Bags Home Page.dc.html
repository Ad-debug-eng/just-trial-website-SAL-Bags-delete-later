About Us

SAL Bags began in a small studio in Pune, India — born from a love of honest materials and the quiet joy of a bag that simply works. Every tote, crossbody, and carryall is thoughtfully designed and made to move through everyday life with you.

We believe in slow, considered craft over fast trends — durable fabrics, clean stitching, and details that last for years, not seasons. Proudly designed and crafted in Pune.
