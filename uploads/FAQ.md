# FAQ & Help

## About Us

**Where are you based?**
SAL Bags is proudly based in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Every bag is thoughtfully designed with attention to detail and shipped across India. We are also working towards serving customers worldwide.

**What materials are your bags made from?**
Our bags are crafted using carefully selected premium fabrics such as cotton, quilted cotton, canvas, corduroy, and other durable textiles, paired with high-quality linings and sturdy hardware. We focus on creating bags that are stylish, lightweight, functional, and made to last.

**Are your bags handmade?**
Yes. Every SAL Bag is thoughtfully handcrafted by skilled artisans. Since each piece is handmade, slight variations in stitching, texture, or pattern placement make every bag unique.

**How do I care for my bag?**
To keep your bag looking beautiful:
- Spot clean with a soft damp cloth.
- Hand wash gently if required (only for washable fabric styles).
- Do not bleach or use harsh detergents.
- Air dry naturally away from direct sunlight.
- Store in a cool, dry place when not in use.

## Shipping

**How long does it take to ship my order?**
Orders are usually processed within 2-4 business days.

Estimated delivery:
- Maharashtra: 4-6 business days minimum
- Rest of India: 7-9 business days minimum
- International orders: Delivery time depends on the destination country.

You'll receive a tracking link once your order has been dispatched.

**Do you ship across India?**
Yes! We deliver to most locations across India through trusted courier partners.

**Do you ship internationally?**
Yes, international shipping is available for selected countries. Shipping charges and delivery times are calculated at checkout based on your destination.

**Is shipping free?**
We offer free shipping on orders above ₹3,000. Shipping charges (if applicable) are calculated automatically during checkout.

## Returns & Exchanges

**What is your return policy?**
We accept returns only for products that arrive damaged, defective, or incorrect. As most of our products are handmade and produced in limited quantities, we do not accept returns for change of mind. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with clear photos if you receive a damaged product.

**Can I exchange my bag?**
Yes, exchanges are available only for damaged or incorrect items, subject to stock availability.

**Do your bags come with a warranty?**
We stand behind the quality of our craftsmanship. If you experience any manufacturing defect within 30 days of receiving your order, please reach out to us and we'll do our best to resolve the issue. Please note that normal wear and tear, accidental damage, or improper care are not covered.

**Can I cancel or modify my order?**
Orders can be cancelled or modified within 12 hours of placing them. Once your order has been processed or shipped, changes may not be possible.

**Do you accept bulk or custom orders?**
Yes! We love creating custom pieces and also accept bulk orders for gifting, events, weddings, and corporate requirements. Please contact us for pricing and timelines.
